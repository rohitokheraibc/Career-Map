SC Reservation Guide

Your constitutional rights
are real advantages

Use every benefit available to you — practically and confidently

You are not behind — you have a headstart in many places

As an SC category student, you have reserved seats at IITs, NITs, AIIMS, NLUs, and Central Universities. Lower cutoffs, fee waivers, age relaxation in UPSC, and multiple scholarships. This is your right — use it fully.

🏫

IIT / NIT Seats

15% reserved seats. SC cutoff is significantly lower. Real chance at top engineering colleges with good JEE preparation.

🏥

AIIMS / Medical

15% seats reserved in NEET. SC cutoff ~50th percentile vs ~90th for general. Major medical opportunity.

💰

Fee Waiver

Full or partial fee waiver at most government colleges — IITs, NITs, Central Universities all included.

📜

Post-Matric Scholarship

Up to ₹2 lakh/year from Ministry of Social Justice. Apply on scholarships.gov.in (NSP portal).

⚖️

UPSC Advantages

5 extra attempts + 5 years age relaxation + lower prelims cutoff. More chances to clear India's toughest exam.

🏠

Hostel Priority

Free or subsidized hostels at most central universities and IITs. No need to pay for accommodation.

⚖️

NLU Law Seats

15% reserved at all National Law Universities. Real path to becoming a top lawyer even at lower CLAT scores.

🎓

Dr. Ambedkar Scholarship

State-level scholarship covering tuition, books, and living expenses for meritorious SC students.

🏦

Free Coaching Scheme

Government-funded free coaching for SC students for UPSC, SSC, Banking, and Professional exams.
